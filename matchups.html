Zed
A relatively easy matchup, you just have to dodge Q everytime and stay slightly out of his W range. Never use your R first if you are even in resources.
Ahri
Easy matchup, avoid her E or go in as soon as she used her Q as she will lack the damage to kill you even with her charm. Build Edge of Night for spellshield and Serylda for slows.
Yasuo
You have to be careful with this matchup, early on, you won't win trades with Yasuo. Especially if he's corny and has bone plating. His shield will ignore your initial damage and his W can block both your W and R. Make sure to get rid of his Passive and W first.
Lucian
This is a matchup you will never win. Unless your enemy is incredibly stupid. The only thing you can do against this matchup is pray your jungler camps your lane. Doran's Shield and Second Wind can help with laning. It's much better if you just roam post level 6.
Lissandra
Ban. You will never kill her post 6. Get fed somewhere else or at least help your team get fed. Unless her R is on cooldown, you will never kill her.
Lux
She has unlimited mana and low cooldowns. If you can sidestep abilities then you are fine. Hide behind your minions and stay out of her skill range. If you get caught even once, you will die. Go in once she misses her Q.
Zoe
A single mistake from either party will result in a kill. Make sure to keep your distance from the wave when she winds her Q up. If you hit her with both parts of W go all in. Do not get hit by her E.
Galio
Decent matchup, just avoid his Q and W. You can oneshot him post 6 if he goes for full mage items. You deal 0 magic damage which is what he's good at negating. If he builds armor, go find another target.
Irelia
Cancer matchup, you can never burst her. Go max W and push roam on repeat. If you fail your combo and she manages to live, you are dead.
